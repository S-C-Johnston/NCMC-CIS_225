\begin{description}
	\item [Too much money:] no feedback is given when providing
		greater than exact change.
	\item [Refund:] no refund is given on an incorrect amount being
		provided to the ticket machine.
	\item [Insufficient funds:] no check is made for insufficient
		funds being given, and it just prints a ticket and zeros
		the balance.
\end{itemize}
